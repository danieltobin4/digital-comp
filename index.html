Daniel's Project

An audio-visual journey through the nature of Pittsburgh

Project

This project was created to explore the human affect on the environment. Using music, I wanted to explore how you can create emotion and messages with just different sounds.

Technical

This project was completed using Final Cut and Logic Pro X. The music was composed on Logic Pro X with a midi keyboard and an audio interface for guitar. It was then paired with the footage in Logic Pro. The goal was to highlight the effect that humans do to our Earth and its environments.

Impact

This project was made due to my anxiety about the future of our planet and civilizations. With the growing change in the climate and the seemingly little regard to stop that, I wanted to find a way to put that into a piece of media. I hope that it has the same feeling that I feel about the issue and helps the audience experience it as well.

Moving Forward

During the making of this project it helped me learn more about filmmaking because that is not normally a field that I am experienced in. I learned about shots and different editting skills and hope to further that knowledge in the future by taking on more projects that push that knowledge. I also hope to get more experience making music for videos. That was something that I had never done before and found quite difficult. There was a lot of starting over and scratching of ideas because it was difficult to correctly express a video through music. I think it is somehting that I will definitely experiment with more in the future.
